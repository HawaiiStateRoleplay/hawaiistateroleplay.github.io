Rules & Regulations

Hawaii State Roleplay relies on rules and regulations to thrive. Failure to abide by any rules will result in moderation. Click the dropdown boxes below to see the Discord and Roblox rules.

Respect Members

1a. Be kind, respectful of ideas and opinions. Not everything has to be an argument.

Keep Arguments Minimal

2a. Keep it polite and if it gets too crazy, you may be moderated. If arguments get too heated, users should either move to DMs or stop entirely.

Minimal Pings

You should not ping someone more than 2 times to get their attention at once.

Mature Voice Chats

This means no inappropriate conversations, inappropriate video feeds, etc.

Privacy Protection

Privacy should be given to every Hawaiian. Doxxing is strictly prohibited.

Hacking and Modifications

Hacking and modded clients are strictly prohibited.

Automod Bypassing

Bypassing the automod is strictly prohibited.

Correct Channel Usage

Use channels how they are meant to be used. The media channel should be for media, not talking about pets. If you see yourself going off track, just continue that conversation into the main chat channel.

Terms of Service

Breaking Roblox ToS is strictly prohibited.

Fail Roleplay (FRP)

Fail roleplay is not allowed. This means not roleplaying crashes and not roleplaying in-game movements via text like "-grabs-" or "-cuffs".

Cuff Rushing

Cuff rush is not allowed. This means you must say "cuffs", or something along those lines, before cuffing someone in game.

Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

Vehicle deathmatch is not allowed. This means slamming into people on purpose, running into pedestrians on purpose, etc.

New Life Rule (NLR)

New life rule is enforced. This means if you die, you do not remember what happened before you died. Act as if you actually died and do not know what happened before you respawned. Do not go back to your scene after dying.

GTA Driving (GTAD)

GTA driving is not allowed. This means disregarding road rules, driving in oncoming traffic, speeding, and acting like you do not know how to drive.

Banned Vehicles

Banned vehicles are for supporters only. Supporters are people who boost the server. A shop will be made with more options to become a supporter. Supporter only cars are cars that have the "prestige" tag.

Moderation Calls

The !mod command should be used responsibly. Matters that require a moderator include an FRP player, someone who killed you, etc. Matters that do not require moderators include "this cop pulled me over for no reason". That is not a valid moderation request.

General Respect

Be respectful to all players at all times.
