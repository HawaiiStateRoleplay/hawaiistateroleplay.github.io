Why Join the Staff Team?

Being part of the Hawaii State Roleplay staff team means ensuring our community remains a safe, fair, and highly immersive environment for everyone. We look for mature, active individuals who can handle rule infractions calmly, host engaging sessions, and assist players professionally.

Application Requirements:

  • Must be a member of our Discord server.
  • Must have a clear understanding of all Server and In-Game rules.
  • Must provide accurate account identifiers.
  • The use of AI (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) to write application responses is strictly prohibited. Detection will result in an immediate blacklist.
